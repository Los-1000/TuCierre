# TuCierre — Design Context

## Design Context

### Users
Real estate brokers (corredores inmobiliarios) in Peru, ages 25–50. Mobile-first: 80% of sessions happen on-the-go during client visits, field trips, and property tours. They manage notarial transactions (escrituras, poderes, hipotecas) on behalf of clients. Their core job: get a transaction started, tracked, and closed fast — without calling the notary every day.

### Brand Personality
**Three words**: Confiable, Veloz, Profesional.

The benchmark is Wise/Revolut applied to notarial services in Peru: a product that feels modern and trustworthy like a fintech, but is clearly built for legal/real-estate work. Not corporate-boring, not startup-playful — confident and precise.

Anti-patterns: colorful marketplace aesthetics, emoji icons, excessive decoration, warm/cozy tones, startup gradients, glassmorphism.

### Aesthetic Direction
**Primary brand**: Deep royal navy `#020952` — the exact blue from the logo. This is the foundation.
**Accent/CTA**: `#2855E0` — bright derived blue, lighter hue family. Used for primary buttons, active nav, progress bars, links.
**Background**: `#F5F7FF` — cool off-white with a barely-visible blue tint. Creates subconscious brand cohesion without feeling dark.
**Foreground/text**: `#020952` for headings, `#1A2050` for body, `#6B7A9A` for muted (blue-tinted neutrals — never warm gray).
**Surface**: `#FFFFFF` for cards and modals.
**Border**: `#E0E4F0` — cool-tinted, not warm beige.

**Tier system** (keep): Bronce `#B5540E`, Plata `#6B7F99`, Oro `#C9880E` — these are functional colors with clear semantic meaning.
**Status colors** (keep): The existing green/amber/red/blue status badge system is correct.

**Typography**: Inter (existing). Body text minimum 16px on mobile. Reference codes (TC-XXXX) always monospace. Prices always tabular-nums.

**Logo**: Two-line wordmark — "tucierre" / "●com" — in Inter Black (weight 900) on `#020952` navy block (badge variant) or white text on dark backgrounds (light variant).

**Light mode only.** Dark mode is out of scope and inconsistent with the Wise/Revolut benchmark.

**Logo file**: `/public/logo.svg` — navy badge variant. Use `next/image` or the `<Logo>` component from `src/components/ui/Logo.tsx`.

### Design Principles
1. **Navy leads, blue accent follows** — `#020952` is the dominant brand color. The CTA accent `#2855E0` is a derivative, not a competitor. Every page should feel "navy blue first."
2. **Cool-tinted neutrals only** — Muted text, borders, and backgrounds use blue-tinted grays (`#6B7A9A`, `#E0E4F0`, `#F5F7FF`). Warm grays (slate, zinc, stone) are off-brand.
3. **Mobile-first precision** — 80% of users are on phones in the field. Touch targets ≥ 44px, no small tap areas, bottom nav always accessible.
4. **Trust through restraint** — Brokers are managing legal documents. Excessive animation, color, or decoration undermines credibility. Motion is used only for state transitions, never decoration.
5. **Fintech clarity** — Numbers, prices, and statuses are always formatted correctly (tabular-nums, monospace codes, clear status badges). Information density is high but never cluttered.
