# TuCierre Site Vision

## 1. Product Overview
TuCierre is a notarial SaaS portal for real estate brokers in Peru. It connects brokers with Lima notaries digitally — replacing WhatsApp-based workflows. Free for brokers; revenue via notarial commission per transaction.

**Tagline:** "No persigas a nadie. Solo cierra."
